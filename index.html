The Tasmanian Long Drinks are produced by a Tasmanian father & son duo, Graham and Riku. They manufacture the product in a home-based distillery in Hobart. The idea for creating the Tassie Long Drink came from the family’s connection to Finland where Riku’s mother was born and raised. One of her favourite drinks is a Finnish Long Drink: a grey sparkling gin and grapefruit cocktail soda served on ice. This legendary drink was created for the 1952 Helsinki Olympics and remains a top-selling product in Finland.

The family moved between Tasmania and Finland and for example Riku spent nine months doing his national service in Finland. During the pandemic, the family could not visit Finland and during that time a spark of inspiration was ignited: why not bring a bit Finnish culture to Australia but with a Tasmanian twist! Graham was already a partner in a small batch spirit making group and was excited to pass on his spirit making skills to his son.

The pair embarked to make the first Tasmanian Long Drink of artisan gin with hand-blended Tasmanian botanicals and all natural ruby grapefruit juice with a hint of raspberries. This refreshing drink is now available in Australia and will also be a stable product in the family fridge.

These men know how to keep the matriarch of the family happy!
